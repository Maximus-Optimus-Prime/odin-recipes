Air fryer Indian tandoori chicken

Lightly charred with a delicious tandoor flavour, it's hard to believe that this chicken dinner is cooked in the air fryer! To pack an extra punch of flavour, you can marinade the chicken up to 1 day beforehand.

Ingredients :

  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 260g (1 cup) natural yoghurt
  • 155g (1/2 cup) tandoori paste
  • Vegetable oil, to grease

Steps :

  1. Cut 2 deep slits into the thickest part of each 8 chicken drumsticks. Combine the 260g (1 cup) natural yoghurt and 155g (1/2 cup) tandoori paste in a large bowl. Add the drumsticks. Toss to coat evenly. Cover and place in the fridge for 30 minutes to marinate.
  2. Preheat air fryer at 180C for 5 minutes. Brush the base of the air fryer basket with Vegetable oil, to grease to grease. Place 4 drumsticks into the basket, slit side down, and cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Turn drumsticks over and cook for a further 5 minutes or until cooked through. Transfer to a plate and cover with foil to keep warm. Repeat with the remaining drumsticks.
