Air fryer asparagus

Want to know how to make delicious air fryer vegetables? Here's your guide to tender-crisp, flavourful asparagus, cooked to perfection in the air fryer.

Ingredients :

  • 1 bunch thick asparagus spears

Steps :

  1. Preheat air fryer to 180C.
  2. Place 1 bunch thick asparagus spears in the basket of the air fryer. Spray with oil. Season. Air fry for 4 minutes or until tender-crisp.
