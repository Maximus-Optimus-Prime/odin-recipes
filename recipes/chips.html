Airfryer chips

Are you craving hot chips, but can't be bothered deep frying? By using your air fryer, you'll not only avoid the hassle, but also be met with crispy potatoes in only 20 minutes!

Ingredients :

  • 2 medium potatoes, skin on, sliced into 1cm-thick batons
  • 1 tsp sea salt flakes
  • 2 tbs olive oil

Steps :

  1. Toss 2 medium potatoes, skin on, sliced into 1cm-thick batons with 1 tsp sea salt flakes and 2 tbs olive oil in a bowl until well coated.
  2. Place in air fryer basket and cook at 200C for 12 minutes.
  3. Remove basket and toss well.
  4. Cook for a further 4 minutes or until chips are golden and crispy. Serve.
